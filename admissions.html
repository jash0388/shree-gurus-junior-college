Admission Process

We follow a transparent, simple 4-step workflow to help students secure their seats.

1

Submit Enquiry

Fill out our simple digital enquiry form below, or visit our Pochamma Maidan campus registration desk.

2

Counselling Interaction

Meet our principal and subject counselors to evaluate interest areas, career goals, and board exam targets.

3

Select Stream

Finalize choice of stream (MPC / BiPC / MEC / CEC) and check if Hostel or Transportation services are needed.

4

Enrollment

Submit required documents, complete fee structure procedures, and collect syllabus study binders.

Document Checklist

Please carry photocopies and originals of the following documents during the final campus counseling visit:

  • 10th Standard / SSC Marks Memo
  • Transfer Certificate (TC) from previous school
  • Study/Bonafide Certificate (Grades 6th to 10th)
  • 4 Passport-sized student color photographs
  • Aadhaar Card copy (Student & Parent)
  • Caste/Category Certificate (if applicable)

Important Note

Admissions are subject to seat availability in individual stream batches. Early registrants receive preferred classroom batch shifts (morning/afternoon schedules).
