Intermediate Course Streams

We offer board curricula approved by the Board of Intermediate Education, integrated with comprehensive professional career preparation.

MPC

Primary Pathways

IIT-JEE Main JEE Advanced BITS A/EAPCET

NEET Medical AIIMS Prep Agriculture & Pharma

Integrated Coaching Academy

Our structured learning matrix makes us stand out. Here is how we build results year-on-year.

Daily Practice Papers (DPPs)

Every teaching session is backed by a DPP containing structured numericals and concept questions. Teachers review errors on the following morning.

Weekend Test Series

Sundays feature full-length exams mapped to Board layouts (Saturdays) and national standard JEE/NEET/CA layouts (Sundays), building high exam confidence.

One-on-One Counseling

Subject teachers hold specialized doubt sessions. Students with lower test curves receive customized mentoring to bridge learning gaps.

Teaching Methodology

We follow a progressive academic flow to ensure complete syllabus coverage and revisions.

Phase 1: Micro-Concept Clearing (June - November)

Syllabus is broken down into modular sub-topics. Concepts are introduced with digital aids and experiments, followed by solved textbook exercises and basic numerical levels.

Phase 2: Competitive Integration (December - January)

Focus shifts to speed, short-cuts, negative-mark elimination strategies, and solving past 15 years' question papers of JEE Mains/NEET/CA formats.

Phase 3: Grand Mock Series & Revisions (February - May)

Full-syllabus mock tests are conducted under strict exam conditions. Detailed review analytics sheets are shared with parents along with principal counseling logs.
