College Infrastructure

Modern Learning Environments • Comprehensive Amenities

Campus Facilities

We provide a state-of-the-art campus designed to offer safe, hygienic, and advanced learning conditions.

Advanced Science Labs

Our physics, chemistry, and biology laboratories are equipped with premium equipment, glass setups, safe experiment boards, and smart displays to translate theory into hands-on clarity.

Digital Library & Reading Room

A tranquil environment featuring thousands of reference volumes, textbooks, national journals, competitive practice modules, and PC terminals to browse e-learning resource networks.

Interactive Smart Classrooms

Spacious, well-ventilated classrooms fitted with dynamic projector systems, interactive whiteboards, and ergonomic benches to facilitate an engaging audio-visual educational setup.

Comfortable Hostels (Boys & Girls)

Separate residential blocks with 24/7 security, nutritious meal systems, supervised study halls during exams, laundry assistance, and resident warden guidance.

Secured Transport Fleet

A network of safe college buses serving primary locations throughout Warangal, fitted with GPS tracking, speed-limit monitors, and experienced driving staff.

Sports & Recreation

Promoting holistic wellness through dedicated playgrounds, indoor games rooms, athletic training schedules, and regular sports meets to build teamwork and leadership skills.
